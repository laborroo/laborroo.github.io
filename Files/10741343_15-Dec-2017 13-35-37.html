Create New Loan  [UC-161 Use Case]
     Open in Win Client    
Description (Goal in Context) This UC documents the process creating a New Loan by a Verified Member
Primary Actors TopTeam
 ACTR-140 Borrower
Supporting Actors TopTeam
Pre Conditions
Member is a Verified Member
Member is logged in.
Post Conditions
Success end condition
- Loan successfully created
Failure end condition
- Loan not created. No new Loan is added to the System
Minimal Guarantee
-

Flows
Main Flow-of-Events
  1. Borrower chose to create a new Loan listing
  2. System checks if Borrower 's account details is completed and the account is verified.
  3. System request Borrower   to enter Loan Amount, Tenure (in months) and select Reason for Loan
  4. Member choose Loan Amount and Tenure
  5. System calculates minimum Interest Rate and monthly repayment amount based on the Borrower 's Credit Risk Rating, Loan Amount and Tenure
  6. Borrower accepts provided Interest Rate
  7. System displays summary of Loan application to Borrower
  8. Borrower accepts the Loan details
  9. System displays the Terms and Conditions Agreement and asks Borrower to accept.
  10. Borrower   accepts the Terms and Conditions Agreement and the Loan is created.
  11. Use Case ends with Success.

Alternate Flows 
2a. Account is not Verified or Account Details incomplete
  1. System prompts User that their account is not verified or incomplete and suggest to Borrower to verify or complete account first
  2. Use Case ends with Failure.
6a. Member choose to select higher Interest Rate than provided minimum
  1. Borrower increases Interest Rate
  2. Monthly Repayment amount is updated
  3. Resume from branching step.
