Verify Employment  [UC-157 Use Case]
     Open in Win Client    
Description (Goal in Context) Describes the process of a User Verifying the company email address so they can apply for a loan as a Borrower
Primary Actors TopTeam
 ACTR-152 Member
Supporting Actors TopTeam
Pre Conditions
Member must be logged in
Post Conditions
Success end condition
- The Member 's Employment is verified
- If the Member already has Salary verified, then the Member   becomes a Verified Member

Failure end condition
- The Member 's Employment in not verified
Minimal Guarantee
-

Flows
Main Flow-of-Events
  1. Member requests to verify Employment by entering Company Email Address
  2. System sends and email to the provided Company Email Address
  3. Member goes to check email and clicks on an verification link in the email.
  4. System notifies Member that their Employment has been verified through their company Email Address
  5. Use Case ends with Success.

Extensions 
3a. Member doesn't click on Verification link in email
  1. Member   doesn't click on verification link in email
  2. Use Case ends with Failure.
